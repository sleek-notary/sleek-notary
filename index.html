Understanding the Role of a Notary Public

A notary public serves as an official witness to the signing of documents, ensuring that the identities of the signers are verified and that they understand the contents of what they are signing. The notary also affixes their seal to the document, which provides a layer of legal protection against fraud. It’s important to understand the role of a notary public before seeking out their services, as this will help you determine the specific qualifications and experience you need.
